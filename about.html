Our Story

From code to crema.

Founded by a group of developers who love coffee, Digital Espresso blends tech precision with third-wave craft.

Sourcing

We partner with sustainable farms and local importers. Traceable lots, fair prices, and fresh harvests are our non-negotiables.

Roasting Philosophy

Light to medium roasts that let origin character shine—think florals, citrus, and cocoa without the burn.

Quality & Training

Our baristas calibrate grinders daily and keep detailed brew logs. Training covers extraction theory and milk texturing.
